Support

Email support@foodymerge.com. A real person reads every message, usually within a day or two.

If you'd like to check the basics first, here are the things people ask about most.

“I made a purchase but didn't get what I paid for.”

First, force-close the game and reopen it. The purchase will usually appear on launch.

If it still hasn't shown up after a minute, email support@foodymerge.com with:

  • The receipt number from Apple or Google
  • The item you bought (e.g., “100-coin pack”)
  • Your device model (iPhone 15, Pixel 8, etc.)

We can verify the purchase and grant the item manually.

“I want a refund.”

We don't process refunds directly — Apple and Google handle that.

  • Apple: visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find the FoodyMerge purchase, and pick a reason.
  • Google: visit Google Play's refund help page and follow the refund flow.

Refund decisions are at the store's discretion. If you'd like a recommendation letter to support your request (for example, a purchase that genuinely didn't deliver), email us and we'll help.

“I lost my save data.”

FoodyMerge is offline — your save data lives entirely on your device. We do not have a copy of it on a server.

  • If you uninstalled the app, the save is unfortunately gone. We don't have a backup.
  • If your device was restored from an iCloud or Google backup, the save should restore with it. Reinstall the app and check.
  • If you have an unrestored save on an old device, copy the device backup to your new device before reinstalling.

Cloud-save and cross-device sync is on our roadmap but not in the initial release.

“The game crashes or won't open.”

Try this, in order:

  1. Force-close and reopen the game.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Reinstall the app. (Heads up: this wipes your save unless you have a device backup.)
  4. Update to the latest version of iOS or Android.

If it still crashes, email support@foodymerge.com with:

  • Your device model and OS version
  • What you were doing when it crashed
  • A screenshot of any error message

Crash reports auto-send through Firebase Crashlytics (see our Privacy Policy), which gives us the technical details. The crash report doesn't tell us who you are.

“How do I disable rewarded ads?”

There are no ads in FoodyMerge unless you tap “Watch ad” yourself. If you (or a child) don't want to see them, don't tap the button — there is no way for the game to play an ad without that explicit tap.

A parental-controls switch to hide the “Watch ad” button entirely is on our roadmap.

“I'm a parent and…”

We're glad you're here. FoodyMerge was designed with you in mind.

  • My kid is playing — what data does the game collect about them? None unless they tap “Watch ad.” See our Privacy Policy for the full breakdown.
  • My kid bought a coin pack without asking — can I get a refund? Yes. Use the Apple/Google refund links above. If you need a recommendation letter, email us.
  • I want to ban the “Watch ad” button entirely. Not possible in the initial release, but coming. In the meantime, you can disable in-app purchases on the device via parental controls (Apple's Screen Time, Google's Family Link).

Still have a question?

Email support@foodymerge.com and we'll get back to you.
